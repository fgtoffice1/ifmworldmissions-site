Ask Me
Evangelist/Missionary Harley Fiddler

Welcome!

We are so glad you are discovering the possibilities of partnership.  The Bible says that without vision, God's people will perish.  One could say, they will certainly fail!  I believe we have no vision without partnership.  My prayer is that you will join me in an international effort to win the lost for Christ!


Doing Our part to win the world for Christ

My Path and God's Vision to us

I have been traveling around the world, preaching the Gospel of Jesus Christ for seventy years.  I will be ninety years old and will never stop reaching out to this world until I see Christ face to face!  The Lord has enabled me to preach in churches and open-air crusades in many nations.  To establish bible schools, orphanages, churches, medical camps, and outreaches in over seventy nations around the world.  Our schools produce native pastors and missionaries to reach these nations, some do so at their own peril because it is illegal to preach Christ in their nations.  I am so very glad to train and send them out to preach the Gospel of Christ.  We give them training and supply for their journeys.  Join our effort and send someone in your place to these nations!

I Believe

I believe in the inerrant Word of God called the Bible.  I believe that Jesus Christ gave His life that all men could be saved if they would simply call upon the Name of Jesus Christ (John 3:16).  I believe Christ is our mighty Savior, Baptizer, Healer and soon coming King! This is the Gospel we preach to this world!  All our efforts to bring comfort to people are to present this wonderful Word of a loving Savior to them.  We are empowered by the Holy Spirit to preach, and we ask you to join us!

Your opportunity

We are living in the time of Jesus' return!  We all must do everything God has enabled us to do and go everywhere He has opened doors to reach in this world, while we have time to do it.  There is a principle within the Great Commission, Jesus gave to us all.  It asks, "Who will go for me?"   Many are not able to go around the world themselves, but ALL of us can send someone in our place!  Remember, without partnership you have NO vision!  Please, pick up the mantle of Christ and send someone to the nations in your place, support missionary outreaches!  Help us win the lost for Christ!
